About the Show

Join us for the 25th Anniversary of the Kenton Ridge High School Marching Band Invitational! For a quarter of a century, this event has brought together incredible talent from high schools across the region. It's a celebration of music, precision, teamwork, and school spirit!

This year promises to be our biggest and best yet, with special performances, alumni recognition, food trucks, and more.
