About HindSight

HindSight began in 2022 in Beavercreek, Ohio when a handful of experienced players put together their next band. After a few iterations of members and one gig, our beloved drummer spontaneously combusted five weeks after that first gig. We were fortunate to quickly find a new drummer and proceeded to learn the material in early 2023. We rocked a private Halloween Party in 2023 and began booking gigs in 2024 across the greater Dayton area.

HindSight plays a wide variety of Rock and Pop favorites from the 70's, 80's, 90's, and beyond. We cover artists like Van Halen, Green Day, Lit, Gin Blossoms, Def Leppard, April Wine, Golden Earring, Lenny Kravitz, Bad Company, The Cure, The Romantics, Aldo Nova, Styx, Deep Purple, Flock of Seagulls, Eddie Money, Walk The Moon and many more.

Thanks to all the wonderful people who have come to our shows! We add new material on a regular basis to keep our shows entertaining and interesting.
